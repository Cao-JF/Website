關於我們

SEETHATCOW 源自台灣，巧妙結合英文「看頭牛」與台語「是在哭」的雙關設計，以低調內斂的姿態，將在地文化轉化為獨特品牌語言。

SEETHATCOW, originating from Taiwan, cleverly combines its literal meaning "see that cow" with the Taiwanese dialect's pronunciation "xi lei kao"(crying). This unique wordplay embodies local culture through subtle yet distinctive brand language.
